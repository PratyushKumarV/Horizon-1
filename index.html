About Us

Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is a national, non-profit making society registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860. Our aim is to equip engineers of today with the technical and management skills to solve the challenges of tomorrow by learning and being a part of departments such as technical , electrical, management and design. We also organize a plethora of events and hands on workshops aimed at making better professionals, bringing about holistic development of our community.

Workshops

ELECTRONIX.IO

Electronix.io is a hands-on workshop that teaches participants about the intersection of electronics and the Internet of Things. During the workshop, attendees will learn about the fundamental concepts of electronics and how to build and program IoT devices using microcontrollers. The workshop is a great opportunity for individuals to learn about electronics and IoT in a practical and interactive way.

WEB.SOL

This workshop will guide participants through the process of building decentralized applications (DApps) from the ground up, with a focus on deploying them on a blockchain network. By the end of the workshop, attendees will have a solid understanding of DApp development principles and will be able to deploy their own functioning applications on the blockchain network.

AUTOMATE.PY

A one-day hands-on workshop to create a sophisticated weather forecasting system where participants will explore the fundamentals of machine learning to analyze data and predict weather patterns. Incorporate real-time data streaming and interactive visualizations for a fully functional project.

ANDROID.APK

A one-day workshop to acquire comprehensive knowledge and proficiency in mobile app development using open-source APIs. Get a comprehensive understanding of Android development, enabling you to create a fully functional app from concept to deployment.
